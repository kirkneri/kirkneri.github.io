Gateron Jupiter Red

The Gateron Jupiter Red Switch is a linear switch with a light 45±15 gf actuation force, 2.0±0.6 mm pre-travel, and a total travel distance of 4.0 mm. It offers a soft and smooth keystroke for a comfortable typing experience. With a lifespan of up to 80 million cycles, it's a durable option for mechanical keyboard enthusiasts. Notably, it features a softer backlight effect, stable 5 pins, and comes factory pre-lubed for a smoother keystroke.

Gateron Jupiter Brown

The Gateron Jupiter Brown Switch is a tactile switch with a 55±15 gf actuation force, 2.0±0.6 mm pre-travel, and a total travel distance of 4.0 mm. It provides a satisfying tactile bump for precise typing, making it a great choice for those who prefer feedback in their keystrokes. With a long lifespan of up to 80 million cycles, it offers durability for prolonged use in mechanical keyboards. Additionally, it features a softer backlight effect, stable 5 pins, and comes factory pre-lubed for enhanced performance.

Gateron Jupiter Banana

The Gateron Jupiter Banana Switch, a tactile variant, boasts a 59±10 gf actuation force, 2.0±0.6 mm pre-travel, and 3.4 mm total travel. It delivers a crisp, early tactile bump with a two-stage spring for enhanced feedback and durability up to 80M cycles. Notably, it features a softer backlight effect, stable 5 pins, and comes factory pre-lubed for a smoother and more responsive typing experience. Ideal for those seeking a precise and robust typing experience.

The Double-Gasket Advantage. Enhanced Sound and Comfort.

Elevate your typing experience with Keychron's innovative double-gasket mount design featured in the KiRKEY K3. This unique approach combines dampened sound and elasticity, providing a new level of key sounds and comfort. It preserves the flexibility of the gasket structure while enhancing the overall typing sound, delivering a truly satisfying and immersive keyboard experience.

Double-Shot PBT Keycaps. Premium Durability.

Transform your typing experience with the KiRKEY K3's double-shot PBT keycaps, embracing the beloved Cherry profile. These high-quality keycaps are meticulously crafted from oil-resistant, non-backlight shine-through PBT material, guaranteeing enduring durability and ergonomic comfort. Delight in prolonged usage and an ergonomic typing angle courtesy of this elite keycap set.

Programmable Knob. Customizable Control.

Enhance your gaming rig with the KiRKEY K3's aluminum rotary encoder. This versatile feature enables effortless customization of the knob for assigning key or macro commands, such as zooming in/out, screen brightness adjustment, volume control, and more. Simplify your gaming experience with tailored controls at your fingertips.

Remap and Customize. Total Key Control.

The KiRKEY K3 offers dynamic key customization through its QMK/VIA software. Remap keys, create macros, and store settings seamlessly. Connect via cable, utilize VIA software to reconfigure keys on macOS, Windows, or Linux. Check out the tutorial video for easy transformation of the M1 key into a Sleep key (Win + L) for added convenience.

Hot-Swappable. Customize On-the-Fly.

Achieve a personalized typing experience with ease on the Kir-key K3. Hot-swap switches effortlessly, supporting a wide range of 3-pin and 5-pin MX style mechanical switches, including Cherry, Gateron, Kailh, Panda, and more. No soldering is needed, enabling you to customize your typing feel in seconds.

South-Facing RGB. Vibrant Keycap Customization.

The KiRKEY K3 boasts south-facing RGB lighting designed to enhance the visibility of non-shine-through PBT keycaps. Offering 22 RGB backlight settings, you can refine RGB hues, brightness, effect speed, modes, and even remap keys using QMK/VIA for a tailored experience.

Screw-In Stabilizers. Enhanced Stability.

Experience smooth and stable typing on the Kir-key K3 thanks to its built-in PCB-mounted screw-in stabilizers. These stabilizers reduce wobbliness on larger keys like the space bar, shift, enter, and delete. If desired, you can even customize your preferred third-party stabilizers for further personalization.
